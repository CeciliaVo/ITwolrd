Project Idea

OVERVIEW

 Role-play games (RPGs) have never been out-fashion games as the innovation of technologies, RPGs have developed into diverse play styles such as JRPG or MMORPG, which attract thousands of gamers. These advanced playstyles have common features which interest players, especially in the 4.0-era when everyone can get their smart device without much difficulty. The gameplay styles aim to the interaction between players such as Player vs Player (PvP) and Player vs Environment (PvE) as their major direction to the game function. On the other hand, graphic designs, sound, and skills effects can directly affect the player's attitude toward the game. However, not all the games can meet all the players needs as there are still several common errors. Therefore, I want to make an MMOR game based on the basic characteristics of RPGs with a combination of popular playstyles.

MOTIVATION

 

This game project is taken inspiration from an animation called Sword Art Online the animation about a fantasy world game using VR technology. These games have a huge number of customers compared to any area on the market. A game that has proved MMO becoming appealed to players is Genshin Impact. In the first half of 2021, Genshin has gained a huge revenue, which is estimated up to $174 million [1]. While the game title was ready to release, the game attracted 21 million players to register. It can be said that miHoYo- Genshin Impact s studio has created a blockbuster game title for RPGs in history. Although the fascinating playstyles, the game has a significant constraint on the operating system to get the game installed. As another MMORPG game title that I have experienced for more than a year is Tale of Wind (ToW) whose studio is Neocraft. This game title also attracts millions of players with creative playstyles including PvP, PvE as well as Social Features (friends and marriage system). The only disadvantages reported by players from this game are time-consuming and unbalance power between free to play (f2p) and pay to win (p2w) gamers. Learn from miHoYo and Neocraft's flaws, this project will generate an MMORPG game title of a virtual world that promises to take the gamers to the best experiences while playing.

DESCRIPTION

The game title will keep the RPG's traditional crucial features which are PvP and PvE. The main playstyle of the game is to get a character level up and become stronger to fight the monsters as well as bosses in dungeons. The games graphic will be in 3D which gets the players a total view of the designs. Alternatively, game characters including NPCs are built-in anime style to attract gamers of all ages.

Class

There will be several general classes by which players are asked to choose before playing. Classes can be subdivided into more typical classes in order to make the game more tempting to experience. A player is allowed to change class at a specific level if they meet the game requirements in resource for the alter.

 

Storyline

This will be covered at the game tutorials as well as missions. Although as a gamer position, I can say that most of the players will skip this, there will be some constraints on which stories can be ignored to avoid players missing crucial instructions.

 

PVE

Most of the time players will fight in dungeons to get resources to get their character stronger.

  Gear realm:

       In this type of dungeon, players are demand to fight monsters and the final boss. Equipment will be available to find when the boss is killed. Realms can be challenged players if they have reached the demanding level.

       Furthermore, there is a percentage for the player to get drop rare equipment. Rare gears will be unidentified and can be in any class.

  Crystal realm:

       Crystals can be obtained in these realms. Similar to the gear realm, players need to take down the final boss the get the rewards.

       Crystals which will be equipped with gears are essential for gamers if they want to get more powerful.

  Other dungeons can enter the events and clan activities.

 

PVP

  Arena: This is optional for players. However, participants will get honors which can trade some resources on the market.

  Survival mode:

       It can be considered as an event that required teamwork. Players are demanded to get in a squad to join. The squad leader can get a maximum of 7 members. However, to enter this mode, 5 players in the clan are required to get into a team.

       Each season will last 3 months. When the leader of the team starts and gets a match, all the team will be on a map where there are 20 other teams. The gameplay is similar to PUBG/ Free fire instead players will use the class power.

  Clan vs Clan (CvsC): In this event, two clans will against each other in a dungeon:

       In the first round, the MOBA game style will be applied, which means the guild has to take down the opponent turret to gain points. There will be 3 towers in each lane, 2 defense turrets, and the main base.

       The second round is 7vs7 in which pioneers are voted by members of the clan.

 

SOCIAL SYSTEM: To get players to interact with each other. Gamers can add friends and do activities together. When 2 players get enough affinity, they are allowed to get married.

 

FARM: This is a fun extra feature to attract people who love farm games such as Hay Day. Farm decorations can be bought from the shop. Players have to complete orders to gain farm exp and coins.

 

MARKET: Where gamers can trade the rare items that they obtain from the dungeon.

       What is Blockchain?

A Blockchain is a database where data is contained in encrypted blocks then chains those blocks to a chronological single data source of truth. To simplify the definition of blockchain, lets take Google Docx as an example. As it creates a decentralized distribution chain, the shared documents can be accessed by everyone at the same time.

 

Blockchain characteristics:

  Unable to be destroyed or faked: In theory, Blockchain technology only disappears when there is no Internet in the world or can be decoded by quantum computers.

  Immutable: Blockchains data cannot be altered. If it is changed, traces will be left. Which means they will be stored forever.

  Security: The information and data can be distributed and safe in the blockchain.

  Transparency: Anyone can track where blockchain data going.

  Smart contracts: digital contracts embedded in if-this-then-that (IFTTT) code to permit them to self-execute without the existence with the third party.
